Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Unfollowers Track ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Instagram Login Information

When you log in to Instagram through our app:

  • We do not store your Instagram username or password
  • Login is handled through Instagram's official web interface
  • We temporarily access your follower and following lists for analysis
  • Session data is only stored locally on your device

Analysis Data

The app analyzes your Instagram followers and following lists to provide insights such as:

  • Users who follow you back
  • Users who don't follow you back
  • Users who unfollowed you
  • Mutual followers

All this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app, including:

  • App crashes and errors
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Device type and operating system version

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide follower tracking and analysis features
  • Display insights about your Instagram account
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Process subscription payments (handled by Apple)

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All analysis data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain external servers with your data
  • Instagram credentials are never stored by our app
  • Subscription information is managed by Apple's App Store

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Instagram: For accessing your follower/following data
  • Apple App Store: For subscription payments
  • Adapty: For subscription management

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Request information about what data we collect
  • Opt-out of anonymous analytics
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through App Store settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
