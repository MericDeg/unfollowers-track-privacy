Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our social media analytics application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media account to our app, we access the following information with your permission:

  • Account Profile: Username, display name, and profile picture
  • Social Connections: Your followers list, following list, and mutual connections
  • Public Content: Posts, stories, and publicly available engagement data
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
  • Usage Analytics: Features you use and timestamps to improve app experience

Important: All social media data is stored securely and locally on your device only. We never store your social media information on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your information to provide you with valuable insights and analytics:

  • Analyze your social media audience and engagement patterns
  • Show changes in your follower count and identify new or lost connections
  • Display engagement statistics and content performance metrics
  • Provide notifications about account activity (if you enable this feature)
  • Improve app performance and add new features based on usage patterns
  • Process and manage your premium subscription purchases

We never sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your social media data is encrypted and stored exclusively on your personal device. We do not maintain copies of this data on any external servers or cloud services.

Secure Connection: When accessing your social media account, we use industry-standard secure connections (HTTPS/SSL) to protect your login session.

Data Retention: Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Using the "Clear Data" or "Reset" feature in app settings
  • Disconnecting your account

Security Best Practices: We implement encryption for local data storage and never store your account passwords or credentials within our app.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide our services, we work with the following trusted partners:

Social Media Platforms: You connect your account directly through the social media platform's login system. Your use of their platform remains subject to their own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We access only the information you authorize through standard platform permissions.

Subscription Management (Adapty): We use Adapty to process subscription payments and manage premium features. Adapty may collect:

  • Subscription purchase and renewal information
  • Device identifier for subscription validation
  • Basic app usage metrics

Learn more: Adapty Privacy Policy

Apple In-App Purchases: All payments are securely processed through Apple's App Store. We never have access to your payment card information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs how they handle your payment data.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data and can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access Your Data: View all information stored in the app through the app interface
  • Delete Your Data: Remove all stored data instantly using the "Clear Data" feature in settings
  • Export Your Data: Export your analytics and insights data at any time
  • Update Information: Refresh your data by running a new analysis
  • Control Notifications: Enable or disable notifications in app settings or iOS system settings
  • Disconnect Account: Log out at any time to revoke app access to your social media account

For GDPR (European Union) and CCPA (California) Users:

  • Right to know what personal data we collect
  • Right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell any data)
  • Right to equal service and price regardless of privacy choices

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us through the Feedback option.

6. Notifications and Background Activity

Push Notifications: If you enable notifications, we may send you:

  • Reminders to check your analytics (optional, up to 3 times daily)
  • Alerts when analysis is complete
  • Optional notifications about follower changes

Your Control: You can manage notification preferences:

  • In app settings to control frequency and types
  • In iOS Settings â†’ Notifications to disable completely
  • Notifications are entirely optional and not required to use the app

Background Refresh: With your permission, the app may check for updates in the background using iOS background refresh capabilities. This helps keep your analytics up-to-date without opening the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences ages 4 and up. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 4 years of age.

If you are under 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction), please ensure you have permission from a parent or guardian before using this app.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 4, we will delete that information immediately. Parents or guardians who believe we may have collected information from a young child should contact us through the Feedback option.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, features, or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you through the app or via email if we make significant changes
  • Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Data Protection and Security

We take data security seriously and implement multiple safeguards:

  • Encryption: All data stored on your device is encrypted using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • Secure Connections: All network communications use HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • No Server Storage: Your social media data never leaves your device or gets stored on external servers
  • Access Controls: The app uses iOS security features to protect stored data

While we implement industry-standard security measures, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices.

In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting your information, we will notify you promptly in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

10. International Users and Data Transfers

Local Storage: Your social media data is stored only on your personal device and is not transferred internationally by our app.

Subscription Services: Our subscription management partner (Adapty) may process subscription-related data across different countries. This data is limited to purchase information and does not include your social media content or connections.

Social Media Platforms: When you use social media platforms, those platforms may transfer data internationally according to their own privacy policies.

11. App Functionality and Limitations

We strive to provide accurate analytics, but please be aware:

  • Analytics are estimates and may not be 100% accurate
  • Social media platforms may update their systems, temporarily affecting app features
  • App functionality requires an internet connection
  • We maintain app compatibility through regular updates

For the best experience:

  • Keep the app updated to the latest version
  • Use a stable internet connection
  • Follow social media platform guidelines and terms of service

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out to us:

  • Use the Feedback feature in app settings
  • Contact us through the App Store support page

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 30 days. Your privacy is important to us, and we're here to help address any concerns you may have.
